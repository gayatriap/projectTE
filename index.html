how to prevent virus

wash your place

leaning with a household cleaner that contains soap or detergent reduces the amount of germs on surfaces and decreases risk of infection from surfaces

maintain distance

Maintain at least a 1-metre distance between yourself and others to reduce your risk of infection when they cough, sneeze or speak. Maintain an even greater distance between yourself and others when indoors.

don't touch face

to help prevent infections, keep your hands away from your eyes, nose, and mouth especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.

wash your hand

Washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of respiratory and diarrheal infections from one person to the next.

use napkin

The use of mask and covering face using napkin or elbow while coughing and disposing the napkin would add an added advantage. We all know that sanitizer containing good quality alcohol can kill most of the germs, bacteria

wear a mask

The main value of wearing a mask is to protect other people. If the person wearing the mask is unknowingly infected, wearing a mask will reduce the chance of them passing the virus on to others.

take precautions against covid-19

things you should DO

wash your hand

Washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of respiratory and diarrheal infections from one person to the next.

always wear a mask

The main value of wearing a mask is to protect other people. If the person wearing the mask is unknowingly infected, wearing a mask will reduce the chance of them passing the virus on to others.

stay home during fever

Mild symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, sore throat or cough, etc. But call your doctor if you develop difficulty breathing. Doctors also suggest drinking plenty of fluids and taking rest.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

void close contact with animals to reduce the possibility of being infected with Covid-19 from these, the Health Ministry has advised amid reports of animals testing positive for the virus.

avoid close contact with peoples

Avoiding Close Contact (Social Distancing) To Reduce Spread of COVID-19. Human-to-human contact is the most common way that a person with COVID-19 can infect other people.

avoid crowded area

Avoid crowds and poorly ventilated spaces. Being in crowds like in restaurants, bars, fitness centers, or movie theaters puts you at higher risk for COVID-19. Avoid indoor spaces that do not offer fresh air from the outdoors as much as possible.
